Courses We Offer

Intermediate(10+2)

Secondary and Senior Secondary Course, encompassing Class 11 and Class 12 (or 10+2), marks the culmination of school education before students transition to higher education at the undergraduate level. At this stage, students select from various streams including Science, Commerce, and Humanities/Arts, allowing for greater subject specialization

Graduation

The university will also offer undergraduate programs in various disciplines, with the last date for admission in 2024 being September 27, 2024. The admission process is based on merit, and candidates must have completed 10+2 or equivalent with a minimum of 50% marks.

Post-Ggraduation

Our facility

The faculty members at a college are the backbone of its academic and research programs. At our college, we have a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are dedicated to providing quality education to our students. Our faculty members come from diverse backgrounds and have expertise in various fields of study. All of our faculty members hold advanced degrees in their respective fields and are committed to staying up-to-date with the latest developments in their areas of expertise. They are not only experts in their subjects but also possess excellent teaching skills, which enable them to deliver lectures in an engaging and interactive manner. Apart from teaching, our faculty members are also involved in research activities. They publish papers in renowned academic journals and attend conferences and seminars to present their work. This helps them to stay abreast of the latest developments in their fields and also contributes to the overall academic and research culture of our college.

World Class Library

The library is divided into different sections – Acquisition, Help Desk, E-Library, Periodical and Reference section, General Stacks, Reading Halls. The library has subscriptions to most renowned research journals including e subscriptions and one can avail access to e-books/journals within and outside the campus.
